OUR STORY

Stephen and Juliette Wall opened lazeezEats after they travelled to India and fell in love with the food.

In 2005, they made the decision to start a restaurant serving Indian Food while sat on high stools, in streets of Mumbai.

All these years later, lazeezEats is a group of restaurants dotted around the country, serving great value, healthy Indian food that's cooked fresh in each kitchen daily.
